# h-BN Ionic Barrier Physics

## Introduction
This document elaborates on the ionic barrier properties of hexagonal Boron Nitride (h-BN), focusing on its ability to withstand ionic diffusion and its stability under high temperatures.

## Key Properties
- **High thermal conductivity**: Essential for heat dissipation.
- **Electrical insulation**: Prevents unwanted current leakage.
- **Chemical stability**: Resists degradation under harsh conditions.

## Applications
- Used in electronic devices as a dielectric.
- Enhances the longevity of storage systems by preventing ionic migration.
